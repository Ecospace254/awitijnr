I'm a Geospatial Application Engineer and Managing Director at Ecospace Services Limited. 03, About

From fieldwork to systems

I started out in the field, traverses, levelling, cadastral surveys, and progressively moved up the stack: GIS analysis, geodatabase design, web GIS, and finally the CRM/ERP platforms that tie it all together for the organisations I work with.

Today I lead Ecospace Services Limited as Managing Director and Lead Geospatial Application Engineer. I bridge executive direction with hands-on technical delivery, designing data-capture workflows, running drone campaigns, architecting land information systems, and mentoring multidisciplinary teams across surveying, GIS, and software development.

My work has taken me across Kenya, South Sudan, Somalia, Rwanda, Uganda, and Tanzania, delivering for public-sector clients, county governments, and private developers in land, infrastructure, energy, and agriculture.
